========================================================================
             EMAIL SIGNATURE INSTALLATION GUIDE
========================================================================

Thank you for your order! Your professional email signature is ready.
Please follow these simple steps to install it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: PREVIEWING THE SIGNATURE
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open the "preview.html" file in any web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, etc.).

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2: COPYING THE SIGNATURE
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Once the signature is visible in your browser:
   - Click anywhere on the signature.
   - Press Ctrl+A (Windows) or Cmd+A (Mac) to select all.
   - Press Ctrl+C (Windows) or Cmd+C (Mac) to copy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3: ADDING TO YOUR EMAIL CLIENT
------------------------------------------------------------------------

GMAIL:
1. Open Gmail and click the Gear Icon (Settings) -> "See all settings".
2. Scroll down to the "Signature" section.
3. Click "Create new", name it, and paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V) into the box.
4. IMPORTANT: Set the "Signature defaults" to your new signature.
5. Scroll to the bottom and click "Save Changes".

OUTLOOK (WEB):
1. Click the Gear Icon -> "View all Outlook settings".
2. Go to "Mail" -> "Compose and reply".
3. Under "Email signature", paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V) your signature.
4. Click "Save".

OUTLOOK (DESKTOP):
1. Go to "File" -> "Options" -> "Mail".
2. Click "Signatures...".
3. Click "New", name it, and paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V) into the Edit signature box.
4. Click "OK".

APPLE MAIL:
1. Go to "Mail" -> "Settings" (or Preferences) -> "Signatures".
2. Choose the account, click the "+" button.
3. UNCHECK "Always match my default message font".
4. Paste (Cmd+V) your signature into the box.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Desktop Required: For the initial setup, please use a desktop computer.
- Formatting: If the spacing looks strange after pasting, try "Paste and Match Style" or "Keep Source Formatting" options if available.
- Images: All images are hosted securely and will load automatically.
