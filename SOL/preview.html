Quick Copy Method:

  1. Right-click inside the signature preview box above
  2. Select "Select All" or press Ctrl+A (Cmd+A on Mac)
  3. Copy the signature (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)
  4. Open your email client's signature settings
  5. Paste the signature (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)
  6. Save your changes
